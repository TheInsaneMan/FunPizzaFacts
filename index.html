Fun Facts About Pizza

1. The World’s Largest Pizza

The world’s largest pizza was made in 2012 and measured 1261.65 square meters! It was called "Ottavia" and was made in Rome, Italy.

2. Pizza Was First Made in Italy

Pizza as we know it today originated in Naples, Italy, around the 18th century. The classic Margherita pizza was named after Queen Margherita of Savoy.

3. Americans Eat a Lot of Pizza!

Americans consume approximately 3 billion pizzas a year, with around 350 slices eaten every second in the U.S.!

4. There Are Over 70,000 Pizzerias in the U.S.

In the United States alone, there are over 70,000 pizzerias, serving a wide variety of pizza styles from New York to Chicago.

5. Pizza Has Health Benefits

Pizza can be healthy! When made with whole-wheat crust and topped with vegetables, it can provide a good source of fiber and vitamins.
